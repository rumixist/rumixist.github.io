2. How We Use Your Information


We are using these informations to make account system work. We do not share any information with third parties.




3. Cookies

4. Data Security


Your password is hashed. We can not be sure all your data is safe from hackers. Please do not share any personal or sensitive information.




5. Account System


We are using an independent account system that we made only for this website. Your account is not associated with your other accounts. This means you need to make a new account for this website. We do not support third party logins like Google, Facebook, Twitter etc. Also this helps you to keep your privacy.


If you want to delete your account you need to contact with us at support.


If you want a normal account deletion, we can delete all information (username, profile picture, your setting, friends etc.) from your account but we will keep your ID on this account and this account will still exist in out datastore without any of your information (it will be shown like a deleted account)

But if you want a full account deletion, you need to have a important reason. If your reason is approved, we can delete the account entirely from our datastore.




6. Changes to This Privacy Policy


We may update our Privacy Policy. We will notify you when we update it.
